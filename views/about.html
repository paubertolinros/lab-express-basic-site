MO EID

Mo (aka Mohammad) is a Multidisciplinary Designer with an unhealthy obsession with anything tech, design.
Currently based in Dubai where he is crafting pixels at Hogarth Worldwide for a really cool client.
Over the past 15 years he has worked on a diverse range of clients and projects across multiple disciplines which resulted in him wearing different hats and develop versatile skills across digital design, code, motion.
He help brands and businesses build useful products, maximize reach and create meaningful experiences through digital design, code, and motion.
His talent and career are reflected in his meticulous and precise work, where minimalism, pastel tones and unmatched originality prevail.

FOCUS IN: Art direction. Visual design. User Interface Design. Prototyping. Wireframing. Motion desgin. Web Design. Interactive Design. Game design. Illustration
